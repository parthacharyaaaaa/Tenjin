Privacy Policy

At TENJIN, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy outlines what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data Collection

We collect the following types of data:

  • Information you provide directly (e.g., username, email during signup)
  • Usage data (e.g., pages visited, actions taken)
  • Cookies and analytics data

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data may be used for:

  • Improving our services and features
  • Personalizing your user experience
  • Sending updates or notifications (with your consent)
  • Analyzing user engagement and trends

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with third-party services for analytics or hosting under strict confidentiality agreements.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request corrections or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for data usage

5. Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. You can read more in our Cookie Policy.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. Any major changes will be communicated via the site or email.

If you have questions, please contact us.
